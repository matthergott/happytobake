About Me - Eloise Hergott

Hi there! My name is El and I am the banker turned baker behind the creation of HappyToBake. After 25 years in Banking, I decided to change career paths and follow my true passion. I have since worked as a chef for almost two years, received my Food Handler's Certificate, and earned a certificate in Culinary Arts from George Brown College. Now I am excited to share my expertise and baked goods with you!

PLATTERS

My bread and butter, so to speak. These platters are great for parties, meetings, funerals, and much more. These platters come in three sizes, small, medium, and large. I will deliver the platter right to your door on the day of any occasion!

COOKIES

My specialty. A secret recipe with a not-so-secret reputation for being YUMMY, my oatmeal chocolate chip cookies are always a hit. My cookies are an integral part of my platters, but if you want just cookies for your work party, I will make it happen!

LOAVES & MUFFINS

Pumpkin bread and banana bread are my two personal favourites, two delightful recipes that I can bake in muffin form or loaf form, my platters may include either. Life is full of surprises!
