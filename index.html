About Me - Eloise Hergott

Hi there! My name is El and I am the banker turned baker behind the creation of HappytoBake. After 25 years in banking, I decided to change career paths and follow my true passion. I have since worked as a sous chef for over a year, received my Food Handler's Certificate, and earned a certificate in Culinary Arts from George Brown College. Now I am excited to share my expertise and baked goods with you!
